Common Questions

FAQ

Do you offer custom or bespoke glass commissions? +

Yes! We love creating bespoke pieces for special occasions — weddings, corporate gifts, architectural installations, and personal collections. Please contact us with your vision and budget, and one of our designers will get back to you within 48 hours.

How do you package glass for safe delivery? +

Every Luminara piece is individually wrapped in acid-free tissue paper, cushioned in custom-cut foam inserts, and placed in a double-walled outer box. For very large or fragile pieces, we use wooden crates. Our packaging has a 99.8% safe-delivery record.

What is your return policy for glass products? +

We offer a 7-day return window for products received in a condition that doesn't match the listing. If your item arrives damaged, please photograph it and contact us within 48 hours of receipt. Bespoke and commissioned pieces are non-refundable. See our full Shipping & Returns policy.

How long does delivery take? +

Standard delivery within India takes 7–14 business days. Express delivery (3–5 days) is available for an additional charge. We ship to most countries internationally — please contact us for international shipping quotes and timelines.
