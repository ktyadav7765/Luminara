Our Process

The Art of Glass

✏️1

Design

Every piece begins as a sketch — a vision of form, proportion, and how light will play through the glass.

🔥2

Blowing

Molten glass at 1100°C is gathered on a blowpipe. With breath and skill, the master begins to give it form.

🤲3

Shaping

Using ancient wooden tools and gravity, the glassblower coaxes the molten material into its final shape.

✨4

Finishing

After annealing, each piece is inspected, polished, and signed by the artisan who created it.
