Our Packaging

We take exceptional care in packaging our glass pieces. Every Luminara item goes through the following packaging process:

  1. Each piece is wrapped in acid-free tissue paper to prevent surface abrasion
  2. Wrapped piece is placed in a custom-cut foam insert that immobilises it completely
  3. Foam-cushioned piece is boxed in a rigid inner carton
  4. Inner carton is surrounded by additional foam padding in a double-walled outer box
  5. The outer box bears "Fragile" and "This Side Up" markings
  6. For very large or particularly fragile pieces, we use wooden crates
🛡️ All shipments are insured for their full value. In the extremely rare event of transit damage, we will replace your item at no cost.

Returns & Refunds

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Our returns policy is as follows:

Eligible Returns

  • Items received in a damaged or defective condition
  • Items significantly different from their description or photographs
  • Wrong item received

Return Process

  1. Contact us within 7 days of receiving your order at [your@email.com]
  2. Provide your order number, a description of the issue, and photographs
  3. We will assess your claim within 24 business hours
  4. If approved, we will arrange a pickup at no cost to you
  5. Refund or replacement will be processed within 7 business days of receiving the return

Non-Returnable Items

  • Bespoke and commissioned pieces
  • Items damaged after delivery due to customer handling
  • Items returned more than 7 days after delivery
  • Items without original packaging
💬 For any questions about our returns policy, please contact us. We aim to resolve all issues promptly and fairly.
